The ISIC dataset is a collection of images used for skin cancer research. It's created by the International Skin Imaging Collaboration (ISIC), a group focused on reducing skin cancer deaths through digital imaging. The dataset includes thousands of labeled images of skin lesions, helping researchers develop computer-aided diagnosis tools for skin cancer.
